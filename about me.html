ABOUT ME 🌷

Hi! My name is Ainul Mardhiyah binti Mohd Yusof, or you can call me Ainul. I am 21 years old. I was born in HTAA on 27 February 2004 and live in Maran, Pahang. I have 4 siblings, and I am the youngest. I am currently studying for a Diploma in Information Management from Universiti Teknologi Mara in Kelantan. Throughout my studies, I not only learned theory but also how to apply that knowledge to my daily life.

Apart from focusing on my studies, I also spend time on things that interest me. I like to read books such as novels and surf social media, especially Facebook, to read current news. Although young people rarely surf 'mukabuku', there I learn a lot about world news and gain insights from the experiences of those who are older.
